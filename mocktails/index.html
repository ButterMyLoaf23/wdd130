Swamp Water

This is my very first drink I created. I made this specifically for a Halloween party. I call it Swamp Water because of its resemblance to a swamp's murky water.

Instructions: In a glass add 3 blueberries, 0.5 oz of vanilla syrup, and 0.75 oz of lime juice. Muddle all of these together and then strain the muddled concoction to a new glass. Smoke Bomb

This is another drink I made for Halloween. I call it smoke bomb because of its smoky appearance.

Instructions: In a glass fill it with ice, pour 1.5 oz of violet syrup, pour 2 oz of heavy whipping cream, and finally top it off with sprite.
