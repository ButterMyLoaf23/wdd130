Swamp Water

This is my very first drink I created. I made this specifically for a Halloween party I call it Swamp Water because of its resemblence to a swamp's murky water.

Instructions: in a glass add 3 blueberries, .5oz of vanilla syrup, and .75oz of lime juice. muddle all of these together and then strain the muddled concoction to a new glass. In your cocktail shaker add Ice cubes, 5oz of pineapple juice, 4oz of peach juice, and .5oz of blue curaucao syrup. Proceed to shake for about 15 seconds, Then strain the contents from the cocktail shaker to the glass with the muddled concoction and enjoy.

Smoke Bomb

This is another drink I made for Halloween. I call it smoke bomb because of its smoky apperance.

Instructions: In a glass fill it with ice, pour 1.5oz of violet syrup, pour 2oz of heavy whipping cream, and finally top it off with sprite.
