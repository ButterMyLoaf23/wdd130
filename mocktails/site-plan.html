About me

This About me page will be where I explain why I started doing this and where my love for mixology comes from. It all started when my mom was throwing a halloween party and needed to figure out how she was gonna serve drinks to the guests. I told her I could Bartend for her party. At the time I had no experience Bartending but I quickly learned how to use all the tools and proper methods for making drinks. I spent two months creating drinks and I had a lot of fun doing this and after the Halloween party my Mom threw I got offers from the guests to bartend their parties and within two months I had four birthday parties that booked me to bartend for them. This was a lot of fun I got to meet a lot of cool people at those parties and I got a lot of compliments on my drinks and their presentation. I took a break from bartending for awhile so I can continue school, but I still practice making drinks in my free time and I want to start a business out of this after school.
